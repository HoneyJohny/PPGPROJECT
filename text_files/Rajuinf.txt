Pulse rate indicates Tachycardia
Delta T value indicates slowing down of reflected wave from tissues. This could be due to any blockage in circulatory path
Augmentation index indicates very high risk of stiffening of artery, blocking of circulatory path and Lipidemia. Require urgent checkup.
Stiffness index indicates risk of atherosclerosis, Stroke, peripheral artery disease. Require urgent checkup.
Cardiac Output measurement indicates reduced Cardiac Ouput. This may be due to any signs of blockages or sclerosis. Require urgent checkup.
Vascular age indicates signs of arterial aging which is an indicator of cardiovascular disease. Require urgent checkup.
